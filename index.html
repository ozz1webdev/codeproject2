How to Play

Select a Slot Mashine to start the Game and place your bet.
You can Bet a maximum of 3 Coins and a minimum of 1 Coin.
If you hit 2 or 3 identical icons in a row, you win the amount you have bet.
If you lose your coins, either continue betting coins and try again or restart the page to start over.
